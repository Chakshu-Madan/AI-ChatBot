Company: StyleHub

What is StyleHub?
StyleHub is an online fashion and lifestyle store offering clothing, accessories, jewelry, and electronics for men and women.

What are your business hours?
Our online store is open 24/7. Our customer support team is available Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM IST.

How can I contact support?
You can reach our support team at support@stylehub.com or use this chat assistant anytime.

Is support available 24/7?
This chat assistant is available 24/7 to answer questions about our products, pricing, and orders. Our human support team is available Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM IST for anything else.

What is your return and refund policy?
We offer a 7-day return policy on clothing and accessories in unused condition with original tags. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the returned item. Electronics and jewelry are final sale and not eligible for return or refund.

Do you offer free shipping?
Yes, we offer free shipping on all orders.

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit and debit cards, UPI, and net banking.

How long does delivery take?
Standard delivery takes 3-7 business days depending on your location.

Can I track my order?
Yes, once your order ships you'll receive a tracking link via email.

Do you offer size exchanges?
Yes, if an item doesn't fit, you can exchange it for a different size within 7 days of delivery.
